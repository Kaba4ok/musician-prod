My Story

I grew up in Ukraine, and started my way in music when I was 6. I was lucky to perform music with great musicians, and learn from world known teachers.

Graduate from Tchaikovskiy Conservatory in Kiev, Ukraine with Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Music Performance.

Advantages of working with me

Passion is an essential part of my work with students.

As I play and teach with a huge passion, I like to see desire to learn and play in my students eyes. Though not everyone from the beginning has THAT desire. I believe that passion can be developed. It's special feeling to ignite a sparkle in someone's eyes.

Commitment is the next trait I love to see in my students.

As we all know, every artist was first an amateur. And only hard work and diligence gives the way to a talent. Here I'm talking about practice. I will give you all necessary tools and techniques, I will show you how to practice the right way.

But you are the one who will use it. Your effort and practice is that hard work that takes you to the next level
